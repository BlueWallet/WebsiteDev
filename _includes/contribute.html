Contribute to Bitcoin

BlueWallet is one of the most used products in Bitcoin. Your contribution really matters and it will have a big impact on the future of Bitcoin. Join us in this adventure, Bitcoin is the most important idea of our generation. Let’s keep building!

Powered by open source

BlueWallet is proudly open source. If you are a developer, start with the readme, build your environment and start contributing. If you are not a developer, you can still contribute by reporting bugs, suggesting features, and translating the app into your language. See below for more details.
